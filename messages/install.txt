Thanks for installing!

To load the front-page of Hacker News in Sublime Text, the default key
combination is Ctrl + K (or, if you're on OS X, Cmd+Shift+H, Cmd+Shift+N).

To view an article in your browser, simply click the title. You can also click
on the comments link to load the comments, or the submitter's username to
load their profile.

If you find a bug, please open an issue here:
https://github.com/dotty/HackerNews-SublimeTextPlugin/issues

Or alternatively email me with the issue:  mail@timdavi.es
